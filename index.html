About Me

Hi, I’m Tanisha Williams — a digital designer, visual storyteller, and proud mom of two. I bring ideas to life through web design, 3D rendering, graphic art, and front-end development. I’m currently studying Computer Graphics and Visualization at Saint Paul College in Saint Paul, Minnesota, where I’m building both the technical and creative skills to turn ideas into compelling digital experiences.

This website serves as a portfolio of my skills and the projects I’ve completed throughout my studies. I’ve taken a variety of courses geared toward my degree, including:

  • Graphical Web Design
  • Web Fundamentals/HTML
  • Intro to Computer Graphics
  • Photoshop 1 & Photoshop 2
  • Photography

In upcoming semesters, I plan to take courses like Digital Sound, Digital Video, Adobe Animate I & II, InDesign, 3D Character Animation and 3D Animation Fundamentals to expand my creative skill set even further. I also plan to earn certifications in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

My passion for design and tech started back in middle school when I was customizing my MySpace page using HTML. Around the same time, I discovered Photoshop and got hooked on gaming—two creative outlets that shaped the path I’m on today. That early spark of curiosity grew into a deep love for digital creation—whether it’s building responsive websites, designing in Adobe Creative Suite, or creating immersive 3D scenes in Blender.

I approach every project with determination, adaptability, and heart. My children inspire me to keep growing, and every piece I create is a reflection of that drive. When I’m not designing, you’ll usually find me gaming, animating, or making custom content for The Sims 4. I love learning new tools, experimenting with styles, and pushing my creativity in new directions.

Whether you’re here to explore my portfolio or collaborate on something new, I’m so glad you’re here. Let’s create something amazing together.
